DEATH FOREST REWORK - HUONG DAN NHANH

Ban game da duoc doi tu manor sang survival-horror rung ram.

Loop moi:
1. Bat dau tai xe bi nan tren duong rung.
2. Tim 4 linh kien xe: ac quy, day curoa, bo bugi, banh du phong.
3. Trong qua trinh tim kiem, 2 hon ma se lan theo tieng chan va truy duoi neu thay ban.
4. Co the bam E de chui vao bui ram / goc cay rong de cat truy duoi.
5. Khi du 4 linh kien, quay lai dau xe va nhan E de sua xe.
6. Di sang cua lai, nhan E de no may va thoat.

Cac khu vuc chinh:
- XE BI NAN
- LOI MON TRONG RUNG
- CHOI KIEM LAM
- TRAI BO HOANG
- MIEU CU
- KHE SUOI

Hook thay asset:
- Neu ban import prefab vao Assets/Resources/GhostModel.prefab, game se tu dong dung model do cho ma.
- Neu ban import Assets/Resources/BrokenCarPrefab.prefab, game se dung prefab xe do.
- Neu ban import Assets/Resources/ForestTreePrefab.prefab, game se thay cay mac dinh bang prefab cua ban.

Goi y asset Unity Store free de ban tu import:
- Moi truong rung: Free Low Poly Nature Forest / Free Stylized Nature Environment
- Xe: FREE CAR PACK - Low Poly
- Ma: co the dung model Blender cua ban, hoac import ghost asset roi lam prefab GhostModel
